Lentil & sweet potato curry

Description

This curry is an amazing vegan dish comprised of sweet patatoes and lentils.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable or olive oil
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds (any colour)
  • 1 tbsp medium curry powder
  • 100g red or green lentil, or a mixture
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 500ml vegetable stock
  • 400g can chopped tomato
  • 400g can chickpea, drained
  • ¼ small pack coriander (optional)
  • natural yogurt and naan bread, to serve

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp vegetable or olive oil in a large pan,
    add 1 chopped red onion and cook for a few mins until softened.
  2. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp mustard seeds and
    1 tbsp medium curry powder and cook for 1 min more,
    then stir in 100g red or green lentils (or a mixture),
    2 medium sweet potatoes, cut into chunks, 500ml vegetable
    stock and a 400g can chopped tomatoes.
  3. Bring to the boil, then cover and simmer for 20 mins
    until the lentils and sweet potatoes are tender. Add
    a drained 400g can chickpeas, then heat through.
  4. Season, sprinkle with ¼ small pack coriander, if you
    like, and serve with seasoned yogurt and naan bread.
